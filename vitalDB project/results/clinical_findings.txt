

CLINICAL FINDINGS

1. Physiological waveform-derived features can successfully detect
   hypotension, oxygen instability, and tachycardia events.

2. MAP-based features are highly associated with hypotension risk.

3. SPO2 variability and SPO2 drop contribute significantly
   to oxygen desaturation detection.

4. Heart rate variability contributes to tachycardia prediction.

5. Tree-based models outperform linear models on physiological data.

6. VitalDB waveforms contain clinically useful predictive information.
